UnderscoreJs

- Escape and Unescape

http://underscorejs.org/#escape escape_.escape(string) Escapes a string for insertion into HTML, replacing &, <,>, ", `, and ' characters.

http://underscorejs.org/#unescape unescape_.unescape(string) The opposite of escape, replaces &, <, >, ", ` and ' with their unescaped counterparts.
